Welcome to DavisOS!

DavisOS is an engaging retelling of all the stories embedded in the Davis Centennial Seal. Created by Susan Shelton, the seal contains art and imagery that tells the deep story of the past, present, and even future of Davis residents. The seal itself can be found and enjoyed in the heart of downtown Davis next to Mishka's Cafe!
